Guacamole

A party favorite and great on toast!

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 small onion finely chopped
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • juice of 1 lime
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil

Directions

  1. Peel and mash avocados in a medium serving bowl.
  2. Stir in onion, garlic, tomato, lime juice, salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Add olive oil and lime juice to preference and enjoy!
